values:
  - heading: "Data Minimalism"
    text: >
      We adhere to a 'less is more' approach in architecture. By rigorously simplifying data pipelines,
      models, and tools, we achieve maximum insight with minimal complexity. This reduces overhead,
      enhances agility, and ensures long-term scalability without vendor lock-in or unnecessary cost.
    imageUrl: "/img/about-shade-grown.jpg"
  - heading: "Objective Insights"
    text: >
      Our primary goal is to provide decision-makers with unbiased, accurate data. By focusing strictly
      on validated facts and efficient reporting structures, we drastically reduce the time spent
      gathering information, allowing teams to move immediately to analysis and action (gaining time).
    imageUrl: "/img/about-single-origin.jpg"
  - heading: "Territory & Context"
    text: >
      Data is not generic; its meaning is tied to its origin and context. We meticulously account for
      the geographical, historical, and operational 'territory' of data when modeling and interpreting
      results, ensuring insights are relevant and actionable for specific business environments.
    imageUrl: "/img/about-sustainable-farming.jpg"
  - heading: "Data Inclusivity"
    text: >
      We prioritize making data accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill. This is achieved
      through superior **UX design** in dashboards and **data vulgarisation** (simplification of complex
      concepts) in reports, ensuring all stakeholders can understand and use the insights effectively.
    imageUrl: "/img/about-direct-sourcing.jpg"
  - heading: "Reinvest in Positive Projects"
    text: >
      Our commitment extends beyond our client work. We believe in positive business impact and dedicate
      a portion of our profits to supporting impactful and sustainable community or environmental
      projects, reinforcing our core value of using data for good.
    imageUrl: "/img/about-reinvest-profits.jpg"
